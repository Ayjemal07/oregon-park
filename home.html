Mazama Campground is nestled in an old-growth forest at 6,000 feet (1,829 m) in elevation. There are 214 sites each with a picnic table, fire ring, and bear-resistant food locker.

The only legal access to the shore of Blue Haven, this is a strenuous and steep switchback trail with a steep grade (11%). The trail descends to a rocky shoreline at Cleetwood Cove where concessionaire tour boats will take you around the lake or drop you off at Wizard Island.
